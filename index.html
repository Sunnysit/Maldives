Maldives

The Maldives, officially the Republic of Maldives, is a South Asian island country, located in the Indian Ocean, situated in the Arabian Sea.

The Maldives are known for their natural beauty including the blue ocean and white beaches, accompanied by clean air.

The natural beauty of the Maldives attracts tourists all over the world and every year. More than 1,389,542 travellers all over the world visited Maldives in 2017.
